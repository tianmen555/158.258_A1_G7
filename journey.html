Recommendations

Bangkok

  • World's first-tier city
  • The capital and largest city of Thailand
  • "City of Angels"
  • Famous attractions: Grand Palace, Erawan Shrine, Khao SAN Road, Siam Square, Bangkok Chinatown

London

  • The capital and largest city of England
  • The center of British politics, economy, finance, culture and education
  • Famous attractions Buckingham Palace, British Museum, Big Ben, Tower Bridge

Gui Zhou

  • The transportation hub of southwest China
  • An important part of the Yangtze River Economic Belt
  • Famous scenic spots Huangguoshu Waterfall, Fanjing Mountain, Zunyi meeting site, Wanfeng Forest, Qianhu Miao Village

Gan Su

  • China's important energy and raw material base, industrial province
  • The intersection of farming civilization and nomadic civilization, Chinese civilization and world civilization
  • Famous scenic spots in Dunhuang Mogao Grottoes, Jiayuguan Pass, colorful Danxia Scenic spot
